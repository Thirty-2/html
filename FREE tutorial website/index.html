About Our Hotel

The History Of Our Hotel

PCD Httls was founded in 2017 by entrepreneur Priya Chandra in downtown Mumbai. The hotel's name combines Priya's initials with "D" for design and "Httls" for hospitality. Starting with 20 rooms, PCD Httls quickly gained popularity for its modern amenities, eco-friendly practices, and personalized service. By 2020, the hotel expanded to 50 rooms and received several awards for sustainability, customer service, and innovative design, solidifying its reputation as a premier boutique hotel.
