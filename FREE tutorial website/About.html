About P.C.D Httls

PCD Httls opened its doors in 2017 in the vibrant city of Bangkok, Thailand. Founded by a group of friends - Prapha, Chet, and Dan - who shared a passion for hospitality and innovation. The hotel's name combines their initials with "Httls" for hospitality. Starting with 30 rooms, PCD Httls quickly became a hotspot for travelers seeking a unique experience. The hotel's rooftop bar, featuring live music and stunning city views, was an instant hit. In 2019, PCD Httls expanded to 60 rooms and introduced a sustainable tourism program, supporting local artisans and community projects. Today, it remains a beloved destination for travelers seeking adventure, comfort, and connection.

PCD Httls' success led to collaborations with local artists, showcasing their work throughout the hotel. The hotel's restaurant, "Taste of Thailand," serves authentic dishes made with locally sourced ingredients. PCD Httls has received numerous awards for its commitment to sustainability and community engagement, solidifying its reputation as a leader in responsible tourism.
